# Hero Background Image

## Requirements

The hero section requires a high-quality background image at `/public/images/hero-background.jpg`

### Specifications:
- **Format**: WebP (preferred) or JPEG
- **Max Size**: 300KB (optimized)
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Content**: Restaurant ambiance, elegant dining room, or beautifully plated food
- **Style**: Warm, natural lighting, sophisticated atmosphere

### Recommended Sources:
- Unsplash: Search "restaurant interior" or "fine dining"
- Pexels: Search "elegant restaurant" or "gourmet food"
- Professional photography of Chez Amis dining room

### Fallback:
If the image is not available, the hero section will display an elegant gradient background (charcoal to burgundy) as a fallback.

### Optimization:
- Use Next.js Image component (already implemented)
- Lazy loading disabled (priority loading)
- Blur placeholder for smooth loading
- Responsive sizes: 100vw
